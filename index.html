Passenger Power: Making the Journey Easy

  • Smart Route Planning: Find optimal routes (min. stops/transfers) with clear map view.
  • Nearest Stop Finder: Instantly locate your closest BRT stop via geolocation.
  • Seamless Paystack Ticketing: Buy tickets online securely, anytime, anywhere.
  • My Tickets & QR Code: Access valid tickets & unique QR codes for easy boarding validation.
  • Route Directory: Explore all available routes and stops visually.

Admin Control: Empowering Efficient Operations

  • Admin Dashboard: At-a-glance view of key system metrics (stops, routes, tickets).
  • Full Admin Panel: Manage routes, stops, fares, users, and announcements via Django Admin.
  • Ticket Verification API: Endpoints ready for validating tickets via reference or QR value.
  • Easy Data Management: Simple commands for initial data loading and fare setup.
